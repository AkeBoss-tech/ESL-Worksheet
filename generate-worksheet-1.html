Level 1

Worksheet

People go to the park to see the fireworks. They lay out their blankets. They sit on them and watch. The fireworks are amazing. Some are big and loud. Others are small and quiet. The fireworks appear in different colors. Some are red. Some are green. Some are gold.

Infinitive Practice

  1. People go to the park to ____ (to see) the fireworks.
  2. They sit on them and ____ (to watch).

Answer Key

  1. see
  2. watch

Past Tense Practice

  1. They ____ (to lay) out their blankets.

Answer Key

  1. lay

Present Participle Practice

  1. The fireworks are ____ (to amaze).

Answer Key

  1. amazing
