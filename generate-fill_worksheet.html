Fill in the Verb

  1. George's best friend ___ visiting him. (to be)
  2. Patrick _____ all the snakes out of Ireland. (to drive)
  3. This is especially true for immigrant students, who __n't qualify for financial aid, loans or scholarships. (to do)
  4. Linda, Barbara, and Jeff ___ sad. (to be)
  5. They ___ plain pretzels. (to have)
  6. He ___ it from a dance contest. (to win)
  7. There ____ a lot of people at the mall that day. (to be)
  8. He also ______ him stores and libraries. (to show)
  9. The two of them ____ feeling bored. (to be)
  10. "Mike, can we go to the mall?" she _____ him. (to ask)

Answer Key

  1. was
  2. drove
  3. do
  4. are
  5. had
  6. won
  7. were
  8. showed
  9. were
  10. asked
