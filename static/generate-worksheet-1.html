Level 1

Worksheet

Clowns are cool. They look funny. They wear face paint. They wear big shoes. They wear colorful wigs. They do funny things. They juggle balls. They make balloon animals. They do magic tricks. They tell jokes. Clowns work at many places. Sometimes, they perform at parties. Sometimes, they perform at circuses. They make people laugh everywhere.

  1. They ____ (to wear) big shoes.
  2. Sometimes, they ____ (to perform) at parties.
  3. They ____ (to do) magic tricks.
  4. They ____ (to juggle) balls.
  5. They ____ (to do) funny things.
  6. They ____ (to make) balloon animals.
  7. They ____ (to wear) face paint.
  8. Sometimes, they ____ (to perform) at circuses.
  9. They ____ (to wear) colorful wigs.
  10. They ____ (to tell) jokes.
  11. They ____ (to look) funny.
  12. Clowns ____ (to be) cool.
  13. They ____ (to make) people laugh everywhere.

Answer Key

  1. wear
  2. perform
  3. do
  4. juggle
